Audio Data Transmission

Why?

  • Send / receive in situations in which other communications media don’t work (or don’t work well)

  • Sort of last-resort because bandwidth

How?

  • Acoustically
    • Simple signaling, for example bugle for military, air raid siren
    • Ultrasonic signalling from TV/Radio to microphone
  • Electronically
    • Morse Code (CW radio)
    • MODEM

MODEM

  • Data over the phone line (or other audio channel)
    • Acoustic Coupler: Microphone to phone speaker, speaker to phone microphone
    • Direct-coupled
  • Old School: Frequency Shift Keying
    • Tone for 0, another tone for 1
    • “start” bit and “stop” bit(s)
    • Parity bit? 0 if even number of 1 bits byte, 1 otherwise
    • 110 bps, 300 bps
